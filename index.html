Registered Breeder

In Brisbane, Breeder Registration is essential to legally breed and sell puppies and kittens. We are proud to be one of the first private Brisbane City Council registered breeders. We are also registered breeders with Dogs QLD and the Queensland Feline Association (QFA).

When buying from a registered breeder you have confidence knowing you will receive a well socialised pet that has been fed high quality food, kept healthy, and raised in a safe and ethical way.

Home Raised

Our adults and babies are raised in a loving family home environment. We do not raise our kittens in outside or out-of-the-way enclosures.

They spend their first 3-4 weeks living in our bedroom and are then raised within our home of 5 children, 4 cats, and 2 Siberian Huskies just outside a screen door at the back of the house.

This allows us to nurture healthy, secure, outgoing, and affectionate kittens with very impressive temperaments.

22 Years Experience

As a small, home-based registered breeder at Everton Park in Brisbane, we have over 22 years animal breeding experience. Previously specialising in breeding only Siberian Husky puppies, we were introduced to the wonderful world of Maine Coons just over 6 years ago.

In addition to experience in breeding and raising kittens and puppies, we also have experience in raising many different animals including fish, guinea pigs, and even cockatiels.
